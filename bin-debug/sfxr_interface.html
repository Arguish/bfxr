	    Keyboard shortcuts: ctrl+C / ctrl+V to copy/paste (cmd will work on OSX instead of ctrl).	    
	    

	    Download source code HERE (todo).  This, as with Tom's version, includes a standalone as3 synthesizer that's easy enough to integrate into other projects.
	    

	    If you experience any stuttering while a sound is synthesizing, if you hit play again it should play through interrupted... .
	    

	    Original concept by the beautiful Dr Petter, and then ported to flash by the darling Tom Vian [this version is based very heavily on his code].  I haven't fully checked backwards-compatibility, but I think things made with other programs should work with this one.
	    

	    If you want to see some of the knowledge behind this stuff, check out this short article on sound synthesis by Dr Petter.
	    
	
	    At the moment the file format is not compatible with other versions of sfxr (thoguh this should load their files ok).
